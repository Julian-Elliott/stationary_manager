# Let me thank Gary, and let me name my runners

**Priority:** P2  
**Filed by:** marigoldPress (cozy games, plays at night)  
**Labels:** characters, cozy, feature-request

---

Gary opens boxes all day for NOTHING (I checked — the runners get $12/day and payday narration; Gary gets neither), and there is currently no way to acknowledge him. This is an HR violation and it is also breaking my heart.

**Small asks:**

1. Tap Gary for a high-five. Cooldown is fine. Once a day he should *miss* the high-five and pretend that was on purpose
2. A once-per-day "thanks, Gary" interaction with zero gameplay benefit. The narrator should find it baffling ("The intern requires no validation. The intern is validated by cardboard.")
3. When I hire a runner for my $30 plus wages, **let me name them.** I'm naming the first one Gerald II, after a dust mote near Deborah's desk that I've grown attached to. If naming is too much for v1, at least give them names and use them in the payday narration — "wages disbursed to Gerald II, who does what one Excel macro once did, but with feelings"
4. It just occurred to me to ask whether runners can be fired. Do NOT let me fire runners. Strike that from the design entirely

People keep coming back to games where the little guys are *theirs*. Make the little guys mine.
