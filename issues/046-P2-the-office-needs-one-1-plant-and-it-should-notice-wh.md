- If I miss a few days it must NOT wilt. No guilt mechanics. The narrator watered it while I was gone and will deny this forever ("The plant is fine. It required no assistance. Do not look at me.")
- Stretch goal: it eventually gets big enough that Gary has to walk around it

I know the pilot is six days long. But I'm going to keep the office open long after Day 6, and I'd like something in it that's quietly keeping track of me.
